Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the festival?

Bring comfortable clothing, sunscreen, water bottle, camping gear (if camping), and an open heart! Full packing list available in your ticket confirmation.

Are there age restrictions?

Be.Spirit is an all-ages event. Children under 12 are free with adult supervision. We have dedicated family areas and activities.

What food options are available?

We feature local organic vendors, vegetarian/vegan options, and accommodate most dietary restrictions. Food trucks and pop-ups throughout the grounds.

Can I leave and re-enter the festival?

Yes! With your wristband, you can leave and return. We recommend staying on-site to fully immerse in the experience.

What if it rains?

Rain or shine, the festival continues! We have covered areas and the energy is amazing in all weather. Bring rain gear just in case.

Is there parking available?

Free parking is available on-site. We also offer shuttle services from nearby towns and encourage carpooling for environmental reasons.
